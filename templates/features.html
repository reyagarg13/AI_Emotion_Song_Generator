Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our AI music assistant

How does the AI music assistant work?

Our AI music assistant uses retrieval augmented generation (RAG) technology to analyze your questions, retrieve relevant information from our extensive music production knowledge base, and generate personalized, context-aware responses tailored to your specific project and needs.

Do I need to connect my DAW to use the assistant?

While the assistant can work independently, connecting it to your DAW through our plugin enhances its capabilities by allowing it to analyze your project's tempo, key, and arrangement for more tailored advice. However, this connection is optional.

What music genres does the assistant support?

Our AI assistant is trained on a wide range of music genres including pop, rock, hip-hop, electronic, classical, jazz, and many more. It can provide genre-specific advice for virtually any style of music you're creating.

Is my music data kept private?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. Your music data is encrypted, used only to provide you with personalized assistance, and is never shared with third parties. You can also use the assistant in privacy mode, where no project data is stored.

Can the assistant write complete songs for me?

The assistant is designed to enhance your creative process, not replace it. It can suggest chord progressions, melodies, lyrics, and production techniques, but the final creative decisions remain yours. Think of it as a collaborative tool rather than an automated songwriter.
